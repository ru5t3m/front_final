About the IELTS Exam overall

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is a globally recognized English language proficiency test.

The test assesses your listening, reading, writing, and speaking skills in English. It is widely used for:

  • Studying abroad
  • Professional development
  • Immigration purposes
